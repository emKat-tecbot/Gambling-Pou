# Gambling-Pou
this proyect is made to teach about finantial responsability and personal finance by way of an educational game. In this game you are in control of a digital pet and you must take care of your pet by being finantially responsable. You must feed it and make it happy by earning coins, which create a series of clases in order to manage your digital pet. To earn coins you must work by answering personal finance related questions, or trying your luck at the casino which can result in you earning more coins than by working or loosing a big amount of coins depending on your luck at a certain game. With these coins you can purchase toys or food which make you pet happy for varying amounts of time depending on which toy or food you buy. You can also save money which will help your pet reach its goals and thus prevents impulsive spending. 

# Functionality
The program allows you to spend coins on items
The pet can eat and play with toys
The pet has a timing system in order for the player to monitor happines levels over time
The program consts of a series of questions that allows the player to earn coins if they get them right
The program allows you to gamble in order to earn coins based on a luck sistem and runs on console

# Considerations
The program only runs on console and is made with c++ standard which means that it runs on all operating systems
